HELLO!
My name is Marina Rikman. I am pleased to welcome you on my website of the pomeranian Kennel PomFavorit. Here you will find more info about my beloved dogs and alumni, the show results, feeding and care tips. I studied cynology, in 2003 I founded my own pomeranian kennel. My kennel is registered in FCI system and all my dogs are compliant with FCI standards.

I am very engaged in rare colours of breed Pomeranian. At my kennel you find pomeranians with a short muzzle, very long and dense coat, round ears, thick paws and a compact body. I have orange, cream, white, black and tan, cream-sable, parti, wolf and red coloured poms. The health and appearance of our puppies are very impotant for me. I breed only with pomeranian, which health has been genetically proven for generations.

In our kennel we pay a special attention to feeding of the dogs. We believe that the natural food is a base for good health and immunity. That’s why we use only natural meat meals, as well as the only natural vitamins and mineral supplements of the highest quality.

My dogs are part of my family and living with me in my house. They play in the garden and we walk daily in the nature. The puppies also learn all household, contact with children and other dogs, so they develop a balanced and healthy mentality.

You can also follow me on social networks. If you are interested in a puppy please contact me on Facebook, Instagram or write me a message per E-Mail or WhatsApp.
