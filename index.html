My name is Marina Rykmanova. I am pleased to welcome you on my website of the pomeranian Kennel PomFavorit. Here you will find more info about my beloved dogs and alumni, the show results, feeding and care tips. I studied cynology, in 2003 I founded my own pomeranian kennel. My kennel is registered in FCI system and all my dogs are compliant with FCI standards.
